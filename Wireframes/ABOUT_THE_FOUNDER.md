### How Project Rolo came to be. 

I created Project Rolo during my career break from advertising. I moved from London to New York with no plan or idea about what I was going to do. Confronting this uncertainty was one of the hardest things I've ever done, and I was constantly battling a thousand anxious thoughts running through my mind. 

Then I thought to myself: *why have all these negative voices in my head, when I could have a positive one?* And thus Rolo was born. 

Project Rolo is grounded in the fact that by helping others, you can help yourself. This is Rolo's intrinsic nature as he encourages you to be the best version of yourself because when you're happy, he's happy. (And maybe more likely to feed him)

If Rolo is able to provide a semblance of the joy and comfort he has brought me for others, then this project will have fulfilled its purpose.
