### Section 4
**Purpose:** 
Explain briefly the technology behind Rolo and invite user to read more or explore the open-source repo. 

**Copy / content:**
Headline: How does Rolo function? 

Rolo lives locally on your machine and respects your privacy. He is powered by 3 pillars: 

**Rolo's voice** comes from a fine-tuned [Gemma 3 4B](https://deepmind.google/models/gemma/gemma-3/) model. A small open-source large-language model trained on hand-seeded examples of how Rolo speaks. 

**Rolo's status** is hardcoded and his mood depends on his hunger, social, energy, and happiness levels. This is combined with his perception of the activity on your screen to add flavor and relevance to his speech. 

**Rolo's memory** is built through ongoing interactions with you. He remembers key information you share (names, events, preferences) and stores this into his memory while he sleeps and dreams.
